Code of Conduct for the Event

1. Idea can be from any of the below themes :

  • Product innovation

  • Marketing or employer branding

  • Technological challenges

  • Business challenges

  • Testing

  • Internal engagement

2. Team size : 3-5. (Not more than 5)

3. Team should be able to articulate a problem statement which they are trying to solve.

4. Finish on time and should be able to present the idea.

5. Prototype/working application will be preffered

6. All jcpenney employees can participate and any employee can be part of any team.

7. Teams have to register with all the team member names before hand.Teams can not be changed.

8. Idea once submitted can not be changed.
