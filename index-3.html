Code of Conduct for the Event

1. Idea can be from any of the below themes :

  • Product innovation

  • Technological challenges

  • Business challenges

  • Testing

2. Team size : 3-5. (Not more than 5)

3. Team should be able to articulate the problem statement which they are trying to solve.

4. Finish on time and should be able to present the idea.

5. Prototype/Working application is mandatory.

6. All associates can participate and any employee can be part of any team.

7. Teams have to register with all the team member names before hand. Teams can't be changed.

8. Idea once submitted can't be changed.
